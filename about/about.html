About Our College

Sumatidevi Tompe Institute of Pharmaceutical Science and Research is a prestigious educational institution situated in Chandur Bazar, Amravati. Our mission is to provide high-quality pharmacy education in rural areas.

We offer modern infrastructure including advanced classrooms, state-of-the-art laboratories, a comprehensive library, high-speed Wi-Fi, and a 10,000 sq ft auditorium with top-class audio-visual equipment. Our faculty and management are dedicated to nurturing both academic and professional development of every student.

Our curriculum combines rigorous academic training with practical experience through industry collaborations and research opportunities. We pride ourselves on producing graduates who are not only technically proficient but also ethically grounded healthcare professionals.
